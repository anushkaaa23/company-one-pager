## 1. Company Overview

### What the company does
Bharat Forge Limited is a global leader in manufacturing and selling high-performance forged and machined components. The company provides end-to-end solutions, including concept to product design, engineering, manufacturing, testing, and validation.
> **Source**: [company_website](https://www.bharatforge.com)
> **Excerpt**: "Bharat Forge Limited, part of the USD 3.5 billion Kalyani Group, is a global leader in high-performance components across sectors such as Automotive, Railways, Defence, Construction, Mining, Aerospace, Marine, and Oil & Gas. With over half a century of manufacturing history, we have the largest repository of metallurgical knowledge in the region. Providing end-to-end solutions including concept to product design, we also offer engineering, manufacturing, testing, and validation."
> **Confidence**: 🟢 HIGH

### Business Model
Bharat Forge generates revenue by manufacturing and selling high-performance forged and machined components and providing end-to-end solutions (design, engineering, manufacturing, testing, validation). It sells to customers in various sectors globally, including Automotive, Railways, Defence, Construction, Mining, Aerospace, Marine, Oil & Gas, E-Mobility, Capital Goods, and Infrastructure. The company operates with an in-house manufacturing model, utilizing facilities for forging, machining, and tool & die manufacturing, and has over 30 years of exporting experience.

### Operating Model
Bharat Forge operates with an in-house manufacturing model, featuring facilities for design engineering & product development, tool & die manufacturing, forging, machining, and testing & validation. The company utilizes AI-powered digitalization, R&D, and automation, and produces a majority of its power from renewable sources (Green Forging). It also possesses the largest repository of metallurgical knowledge in its region and has dedicated innovation centers: Kalyani Centre for Technology & Innovation and Kalyani Center for Manufacturing Innovation (KCMI).
> **Source**: [company_website](https://www.bharatforge.com)
