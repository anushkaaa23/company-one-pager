## 1. Company Overview

### What the company does
Brakes India Private Limited is a manufacturer of automotive components, specifically braking equipment and iron castings, serving both automotive and non-automotive applications.
> **Source**: [company_website](https://www.brakesindia.com/)
> **Excerpt**: "Manufacturers of braking equipment for automotive and non-automotive applications, Brakes India has unveiled ZAP..; Every Single Automotive Player in India is a Customer of our Iron Castings Business"
> **Confidence**: 🟢 HIGH

### Business Model
Brakes India manufactures and sells braking equipment and iron castings. It primarily serves automotive OEMs in India and Europe, as well as the aftermarket, and also caters to non-automotive applications. The company operates with 21 manufacturing locations and has a significant export business, particularly for iron castings to Europe. It also engages in joint ventures.
> **Source**: [company_website](https://www.brakesindia.com/)
> **Excerpt**: "3 Out 4 Buses and Trucks in India have our Brakes; Every Single Automotive Player in India is a Customer of our Iron Castings Business; Every 3rd Car in Europe Contains Parts Made by Our Iron Castings Business; 21 Manufacturing Locations; 200000 Tons of Iron Castings Capacity; TISAX label certified for exports to Europe; Brakes India and ADVICS to Form Joint Venture"
> **Confidence**: 🟢 HIGH

### Products & Services
Brakes India offers a range of products including brakes for buses, trucks, light vehicles, heavy vehicles, passenger cars, small commercial vehicles (SCV), tractors, off-highway vehicles, and ZAP brake pads designed for electric vehicles (EVs). They also produce iron castings and polymer products. Services include QIk Brake Service, Authorized Service Centres, and Brake Maintenance Tips.
> **Source**: [company_website](https://www.brakesindia.com/)
> **Excerpt**: "3 Out 4 Buses and Trucks in India have our Brakes; Brakes India launches ZAP brake pads designed for EVs; Passenger Cars & SCV; Commercial (LCV & HCV); Tractor & Off-Highway; QIk Brake Service; Authorized Service Centre; Brake Maintenance Tips; Senior Vice President & Head, Polymer Business Unit"
> **Confidence**: 🟢 HIGH

### Industries Served
Brakes India primarily serves the automotive industry, including manufacturers of buses, trucks, cars, light commercial vehicles, heavy commercial vehicles, passenger cars, small commercial vehicles, tractors, and electric vehicles. They also cater to non-automotive applications.
